Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I ship items of any size internationally?

    While most items can be shipped internationally, there may be restrictions on certain sizes, weights, or types of goods imposed by carriers or customs regulations. It is important to check the specific regulations and requirements of the destination country. Our team can assist you in determining any size or weight restrictions and provide guidance on shipping options for your specific items.

  • Do you provide insurance for international shipping?

    Yes, we offer insurance options for international shipments to provide added protection. Our insurance coverage safeguards your packages against loss, damage, or theft during transit. It is recommended to consider insurance for valuable or fragile items. Our team can provide information on insurance options and help you choose the coverage that best suits your needs.

  • Is tracking availible for international shipments?

    Yes, we offer tracking services for international shipments. Once your package is in transit, you can easily track its progress online through our tracking system. You will receive a tracking number that allows you to monitor the shipment's journey, view real-time updates, and stay informed about its estimated delivery date.

  • What documents are needed for international shipping?

    The specific documents required may vary depending on the destination and the nature of the shipment. However, common documents include a commercial invoice, packing list, and any necessary permits or licenses. Our team will guide you through the documentation process and ensure that all required paperwork is properly prepared.

  • How long does international shipping usually take?

    The duration of international shipping depends on various factors such as the destination, shipping method chosen, customs clearance, and any unforeseen circumstances. Generally, international shipments can range from a few days to several weeks. We provide estimated delivery times based on the destination and selected shipping service to give you a better idea of the timeframe.
